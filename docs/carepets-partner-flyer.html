Can't Always Get Your Dog to the Vet?

We'll drive them there and back — and care for them at home, too.

More than dog care. Peace of mind.

Life gets busy, and not everyone can take time off for every appointment or a midday walk. Ballage Pet Concierge is a local, insured, family-owned dog-care company — proud to be a trusted care partner for the dogs of this practice.

🚗 Vet Visit Rides

Can't make the appointment? We'll safely pick up your dog, drive them to the vet, and bring them home — calm, careful, and with updates the whole way. We can even stay through the visit and relay your vet's notes.
