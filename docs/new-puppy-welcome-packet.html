Your First-Week Puppy Checklist

Setting up for success
  • Create a safe space (crate or pen) for rest
  • Puppy-proof low cabinets, cords & small objects
  • Stock up on food, ID tag, leash & harness
  • Pick one consistent potty spot outside
  • Schedule your first vet visit
Building good habits
  • Take out to potty every 1–2 hours (and after meals/naps)
  • Reward the behavior you want, right away
  • Keep a steady feeding & nap schedule
  • Protect nap time — overtired puppies get nippy
Gentle socialization (weeks matter!)
  • Introduce new sights, sounds & surfaces calmly
  • Keep experiences positive and at your puppy's pace
  • Handle paws, ears & mouth gently & often
  • One good experience beats ten overwhelming ones
When you need a hand
  • Midday potty breaks while you're at work
  • Socialization & enrichment support
  • Consistency between everyone who cares for your pup
  • A trusted, gentle pro when life gets busy
